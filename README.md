# 📦 Supply Chain Analysis Dashboard | Power BI
### Financial Overview — Samsung Product Line (2023–2024)

![Dashboard Preview]()

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **end-to-end Supply Chain & Financial Analysis** of Samsung's product distribution network across multiple countries and sales channels. Built using **Power BI**, this interactive dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor revenue, costs, inventory, supplier performance, and profit margins — all in one place.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze and visualize Samsung's supply chain performance by tracking:
- Financial health (Revenue, Profit, Cost)
- Customer-wise revenue distribution
- Inventory levels by product
- Supplier lead times and costs
- Sales channel performance (Online, Retailer, Direct)

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📌 **Online channel** leads with **41.39%** of total revenue, closely followed by **Retailer at 40.41%**

- 📌 **Galaxy S24 Ultra** has the highest inventory stock at **25K units**
- 📌 Most suppliers maintain an **average lead time of 12 days**, with Samsung India & Vietnam at 9 days
- 📌 Profit margin stands at a healthy **27.44%** across 2023–2024
